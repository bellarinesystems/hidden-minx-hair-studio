About Us

Our Services

Everything Your Hair Needs, Under One Roof

From a simple trim to a full colour transformation — every service starts with a proper chat about what you actually want.

Cut & Style

A precision cut tailored to your face shape, hair texture, and daily routine — not just what's trending on Instagram. Every cut starts with a proper consultation so you leave with hair that actually works for your life.

Colour & Highlights

From subtle balayage to bold transformations, our stylists take the time to understand the exact shade and tone you're after. We'll talk you through maintenance and aftercare so your colour stays vibrant between visits.

Kids & Teen Hair

We love welcoming younger clients and making their salon experience a positive one — just ask the mums who bring their daughters in to see Annie. A relaxed, pressure-free environment where kids and teens feel genuinely heard.

Blowouts & Styling

Whether it's a Geelong Cup event, a wedding, or just a Friday night out, we'll style your hair to last. Our team will chat through your outfit, the occasion, and the vibe you're going for before we start.

Hair Treatments & Repair

Dry, damaged, or just feeling flat — we'll assess your hair's condition and recommend treatments that actually make a difference. Think of it as a reset for hair that's been through a Geelong winter or too many DIY box dyes.
